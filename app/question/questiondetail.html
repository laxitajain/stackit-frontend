How to center a div using CSS?

Asked by: John Doe | July 12, 2025
css html frontend

I’ve been trying to center a div both vertically and horizontally inside a parent container. I’ve tried margin auto, flexbox, and grid, but nothing seems to work the same across all browsers. What is the most reliable way to do it?

Answers

You can use Flexbox:

display: flex;
justify-content: center;
align-items: center;
Answered by Jane Smith

Grid is also very clean:

display: grid;
place-items: center;
Answered by Alex Lee
